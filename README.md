**ALL IN ONE FITNESS APP**

We live in the modern world where we can electronically track our fitness via the use of wearables,
workout planning apps and diet apps. However, most fitness apps are focused on one of the health and fitness 
niches, leaving out the rests.

The All in One Fitness app changes this. You will have your diet planner, calorie tracker, workout planner and 
periodization app all in one place without the need of paying for a premium price. The App is intended to feature
all these features and bring them to you in a user friendly and beginner friendly manner. Here are some of the 
key features intended for the app:

1. Calorie tracking and diet planning with a huge ingredient variety on offer.
2. Diet personalization based on your health and fitness goals,as well as diet preference.
3. Machine learning model to help optimize exercise and diet to your goals and schedule.
4. Personalized exercise periodization algorithm to ensure you progress in the gym as well as recover well from the gym.
5. Offline access to your diet and fitness programs

6. [FUTURE] An AI agent to help plan everything for you and keep you accountable for your progess
